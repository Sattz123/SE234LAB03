CAMT

The College of Arts, Media and Technology (CAMT) is an autonomous college under Chiang Mai University. It was established to support the strategic development of human resources in Northern Thailand, equipping them with knowledge and expertise in technology and information systems. The college aims to enhance competitiveness on an international level, with a focus on the development of the tourism industry, handicraft industries, and the software industry in the Northern region. Guided by the philosophy of “producing graduates, postgraduates, and doctorates who can utilize knowledge at an international level,” CAMT actively contributes to fostering a knowledge-based economy and society
